Blog?
For the first post here I thought, I’ll go thru how and why I started writing and how it led to creating this website. Writing came into my life quite recently, couple of years ago, I would say. It was when a lot of my friends started to create personal telegram channels, I thought “Wow! This is so cool, I could do the same but don’t really know what to write about…”. Then during more depressive periods of my life, when being in emotional states, I could really understand how I feel and why I feel that way. That was the first time the pen and paper came to the rescue! I started asking myself questions and write bullet point with anything that comes up. I was able to take a step back and look at the inifinitely confusing world inside of me, which did help me quite a lot during a few low moments of my life, but wasn’t enough for me to start writing yet..
